# Hero image

Drop a real photograph here named `hero.jpg` (roughly 2400 × 1400, < 400 KB after
optimisation with mozjpeg / squoosh).

Looking for: a wide, calm coastal scene — sunrise/sunset light, a rod silhouette
against the water, or an uncluttered Cape shoreline. Avoid stock-looking
composites, people in branded gear, or obvious tourist shots.

Until you add one, `components/Hero.tsx` will render a solid gradient behind the
copy — the page still works, it just looks plain.
